About K-hub

Knowledge Hub (K-Hub) in broad terms work as virtual library for scholars, researchers, students and faculties for their requirement of online resources such as e-journals, e-books, e-articles, e-newsletter, case reports, videos, conference proceedings, online sample issues on a single platform. The User/IP based access is provided to institutes as per subject of their choice or as per their research areas.

K-Hub offers a comprehensive compilation of databases covering e-journals, e-books, e-articles, e-newspaper, e-newsletter, case reports, videos online sample issues, article submission for authors. K-Hub also provides information to Jobs, recent news, recent events/conferences.

A Google search engine is enabled which is customized to search the resources as per the subject area of the users.

K-Hub is developed by an awesome team of passionate professionals who are committed to innovating and creativity. Our team is highly trained and has great experience to ensure to provide very best advice and support possible to fulfill your study materials requirements on a single platform.
